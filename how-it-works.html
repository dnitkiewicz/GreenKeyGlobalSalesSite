Why the Core Criteria matter

In early 2026, we defined the baseline for a first Key. The 37 mandatory questions form the minimum for a 1 Key certification. A display Core Criteria only filter isolates them in the portal.

The focused work is roughly 4 to 6 hours. Average completion fell from about 80 days to about 11 days after the redesign.

Plain terms summary

  • Govern it with a signed policy and accountable manager.
  • Buy responsibly with a written purchasing policy.
  • Staff it with a Green Team and written mandate.
  • Train for it with clear housekeeping and operating routines.
  • Measure it with 12 months of utility data and maintenance logs.
  • Prove it with documented evidence for every mandatory answer.

The process

1. Assess

Online self-assessment with multiple users and progress saved as you go.

2. Evaluate

Preliminary rating and gap scorecard. Retake freely before the audit is requested.

3. Audit

Independent verification by Control Union and a certification decision by an independent Certification Body.

4. Certify

1 to 5 Keys, valid for three years, supported by a Property Performance Report.

5. Market

Toolkit, OTA badges, RFP content, plaque, and broader distribution support.

Impartial by design

We set the standard but never audit our own members. Audits are conducted by Control Union, an independent global inspection firm, and the certification decision rests with an independent Certification Body, per ISO/IEC 17065.

Audits typically commence within about 90 days of request. Rescheduling requires three business days notice for virtual audits or 20 for on-site audits.

The rating

1 Key1% to 19%
2 Keys20% to 39.9%
3 Keys40% to 59.9%
4 Keys60% to 79.9%

Any rating is full certification. The audit issued Property Performance Report shows exactly where the points for the next Key live, turning improvement into an assignable plan across three year cycles.
