About Green Key Global

We certify operations, not construction

Green Key began in 1993 and has grown into the leading hotel sustainability certification program in North America, with more than 3,000 certified properties.

Contact the team Explore the business case

Who we are

Green Key began in 1993 and was created by the Hotel Association of Canada, now Hotels Canada. In 2024, AHLA and Hotels Canada formed the joint venture that is Green Key Global today.

In partnership with One Tree Planted, a tree is planted for every new member; more than 13,000 trees have been planted since 2023.
