Built for portfolio leaders

We certify operations, not construction. The program is designed to help a hotel prove how it operates, support corporate RFP conversations, and give owners and management teams a clear plan for improvement without a major capital requirement.

Why the case matters to a VP of operations

The strongest argument is not only sustainability. It is operating evidence, portfolio consistency, and a clearer way to win business in a market that increasingly expects proof.

Demand support

Corporate buyers and booking platforms increasingly look for recognized sustainability proof before moving a hotel forward.

Operating discipline

The assessment rewards practices that help reduce waste, tighten utility control, and improve preventive maintenance habits.

Audit readiness

Independent verification gives leadership and owners a stronger, more defendable case than self-reported sustainability claims.

Why this program stands apart

  • Built by the industry. Founded in 1993 and jointly owned by the American Hotel & Lodging Association and Hotels Canada.
  • No capital spend required. The assessment scores what a property already does and recommends improvement over time.
  • A trusted benchmark. Aligned with all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals, GSTC-Recognized, recognized by Travalyst, and verified by an independent third-party auditor.
  • A clear front door. The 2026 Core Criteria define 37 mandatory questions as the path to a first Key.
  • Priced for every tier. Published U.S. pricing works out to less than $80 per month across a full three-year cycle.
  • Built for growth. The program supports a property from a first Key through a full 5 Key certification journey.
