What Green Key Global does

Green Key Global supports hospitality organizations in improving environmental stewardship, guest experience, and operational excellence through a structured certification framework.

  • Builds credibility with sustainability-focused travelers
  • Offers a practical path from assessment to certification
  • Encourages measurable environmental and social impact

Why the Eco-Rating matters

The Eco-Rating journey helps organizations show progress with a clear, evidence-based path that can be shared internally, with stakeholders, and with guests.

  • Creates transparency around sustainability priorities
  • Supports continuous improvement across departments
  • Aligns teams around a common framework and language

Roadmap to Eco-Rating certification

The certification journey is most effective when it is framed as a phased roadmap: understand the criteria, assess current performance, close gaps, and demonstrate improvement over time.

1. Learn the standards
Review the core criteria, checklist, and supporting documentation to understand the expectations and scoring logic.
2. Evaluate current operations
Use the questions and evaluation tools to identify where the property already excels and where improvements are needed.
3. Build a practical action plan
Prioritize initiatives that improve efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and strengthen engagement.
4. Demonstrate progress
Collect evidence, document outcomes, and prepare for the certification review with confidence.
